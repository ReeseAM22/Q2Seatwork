The Different Types of Dogs

Picture Breed of Dog The Description
Poodle These dogs can come in various colors and sizes. They have unbelievably soft fur. These dogs are also famous in France.
Pug They have large round heads and black beady eyes. They also have flat noses.
Corgi These dogs are loyal to their owners. They're witty and dear to people, especially kids.
Chihuahua They are the type of little dogs and they can only be heavy as 1-3 kilograms.
Siberian Husky They are the perfect dogs for winter. They are famously known for pulling things on a sled over cold lands. Because of this, they have the name called sled dogs.
Bulldog Despite their stubborn look, they are kind and brave. They also have short muscled legs and quite short in height.
Labradoodle This breed came from Poodles and Labradors. That's the reason they have a beautiful mix of both.
German Sherperd They are large dogs who are very intelligent and quick. They are also known for being police dogs.
Shih Tzu Their name stands for "Lion Dog." Their size is pretty small, and a lot of people think these dogs are adorable and affectionate.
Greyhound They are known for their long necks and legs. But not only that, they als known for being quick.
